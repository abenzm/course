Yangtze River Dolphin

The baiji, in the region of Yangtze, is regarded as a symbol of peace and prosperity.

  • Scientific Name: Chinese river dolphin
  • Average Length: 2.4 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 24 years
  • Habitat: The main channel of the Yangtze
